# Job Description
## Senior Claims Handler — Motor Division

**Department:** Claims Operations
**Reports to:** Sarah Chen, Claims Manager
**Location:** Zurich Insurance Hong Kong, Quarry Bay Office
**Grade:** Assistant Manager

---

## Role Purpose

Handle end-to-end motor claims processing, ensuring timely and accurate settlement while maintaining compliance with regulatory requirements and company standards. Act as primary point of contact for policyholders, brokers, and external adjusters throughout the claims lifecycle.

---

## Key Responsibilities

### Claims Processing (60%)
- Process 35-45 motor claims cases per week
- Conduct initial triage and coverage verification
- Manage document collection and follow-up
- Calculate settlements and obtain approvals
- Meet SLA targets: 95% of cases within 5 business days

### Stakeholder Management (20%)
- Communicate with policyholders on claim status
- Coordinate with brokers on complex cases
- Liaise with external adjusters and repair shops
- Escalate to Claims Manager when threshold exceeded

### Reporting & Administration (15%)
- Maintain Excel claims tracker (daily updates)
- Prepare weekly case status report for Claims Manager
- Contribute to monthly Claims Committee pack
- File and archive physical documentation

### Quality & Compliance (5%)
- Ensure all claims meet regulatory requirements (IA guidelines)
- Participate in quarterly audit preparation
- Flag suspicious claims for investigation team
- Maintain 99% accuracy in data entry

---

## KPIs & Success Metrics

| KPI | Target |
|-----|--------|
| Cases processed per week | 35-45 |
| SLA compliance (5-day assessment) | 95% |
| Customer TNPS score | > 60 |
| Data accuracy | 99% |
| Audit findings | Zero material findings |
| Average cycle time (FNOL to settlement) | < 12 business days |

---

## Required Qualifications
- 3-5 years insurance claims experience
- ANZIIF or equivalent certification preferred
- Strong attention to detail
- Proficiency in Core Policy System (CPS)
- Advanced Excel skills
- Cantonese and English (written & spoken)

---

## Key Qualities That Make Me Good at This Job
- Detail-minded: Never miss a field or a discrepancy
- Pattern recognition: Can spot suspicious claims from experience
- Empathetic communication: Policyholders trust me because I explain clearly
- Efficient: Developed personal shortcuts in CPS that save ~30min/day
- Calm under pressure: Quarter-end backlog doesn't faze me
