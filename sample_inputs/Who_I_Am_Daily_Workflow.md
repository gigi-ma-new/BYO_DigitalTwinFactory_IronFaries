## My Biggest Pain Points (What I Want My Twin To Fix)
1. **Excel double-entry** — I enter the SAME data in CPS and Excel. Why? Legacy audit requirement. Takes 30+ min/day.
2. **Status update emails** — Brokers ask "what's the status?" 10x/day. I just copy from CPS and paste into email. Robotic.
3. **Report formatting** — Weekly report to Sarah is always the same template, I just update numbers. 45 min every Friday.
4. **SLA tracking** — I manually count days in my head. Missed one last month. Got flagged in audit.
5. **Meeting prep** — Before 1:1 with Sarah, I scramble to pull KPIs. Always last minute.
