Main learning from experience
  • #ArchitectureFirst — 3 options evaluated; the "obvious" approach was 6x more expensive. Design structure before features.
  • #AgenticThinking — Reason → Plan → Decide → Self-Correct. A learnable, repeatable framework — not magic.
  • #GoodAgentCriteria — Reusability, token efficiency, security, human approval, self-improvement. Without scoring these, agents can't scale.
  • #GuardrailsNotGatekeeping — Citizen developers CAN touch production data — with HITL, PII masking, audit trails, and parallel runs.
  • #ProgressiveAutonomy — Start 100% human approval. As accuracy proves, gradually release. Visible risk + improving > invisible risk + no trail.
Description and quantification of the problem
No one knows how to approach AI — even though everyone wants to automate the 80% repetitive tasks.
  • #StartingHurdle — 95% lack tech skills; traditional AI needs devs, months, $$$. Nothing starts. All they have is unorganized dumps of SOPs, JDs, org charts, workflow diagrams, job shadow recordings.
  • #ExpensiveAI — "AI = expensive" kills every scaling conversation. No one teaches efficient architecture or how agents think.
  • #OneSizeFailsAll — Generic chatbots fit no one; staff try once, quit.
  • #TrustGap — AI repeats mistakes; trust erodes; adoption dies.
  • #ShelfwareRisk — Role changes = tool obsolete. Rebuild = back to zero.
Cost: $31K/year inefficient paid hours wasted per person (80% of work hours on manual tasks AI could handle) × 1,000 HK staff = $31M annual opportunity lost.
How your approach is solving the problem
Digital Twin Factory — one solution, five problems killed.
  • #StartingHurdle → Agent Builds Agents — Just talk. Dump SOPs, JDs, org charts, recordings — any format. 11 agents build your twin in 10 min. Zero coding.
  • #ExpensiveAI → Cost-Smart 2-Layer — Claude thinks, n8n does (85% cheaper than Claude alone). Costs less than a coffee a day. Factory teaches efficient architecture and how agents think → costs drop further.
  • #OneSizeFailsAll → Your Twin ≠ My Twin — Same factory, completely different skills per role. Claims twin ≠ Marketing twin ≠ Finance twin — tailored uniquely to you.
  • #TrustGap → Easy Feedback, Skills Learn — Approve / Edit / Reject each output. Every correction permanent. Accuracy rate rises. Edit rate drops.
  • #ShelfwareRisk → Grows With You — New role? Process has changed? The Factory helps you easily add a skill. No rebuild ever.
